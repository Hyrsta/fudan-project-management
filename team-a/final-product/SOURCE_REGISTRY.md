# Source Registry

## Purpose

The source registry gives the product a transparent retrieval and ranking base. It stores source weights and direct RSS feeds that the Collector stage can check before falling back to Google News RSS.

## Current direct feeds

| Source | Category | Region | Role |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| BBC News | World | Global | Broad international coverage |
| TechCrunch | Technology | US | Technology and startup coverage |
| The Verge | Technology | US | Technology and platform coverage |
| Le Monde | World | Europe | International and European perspective |

## Ranking use

Each article receives:

- source credibility score
- freshness score
- topic-fit score
- total rank score

The UI surfaces these scores so users can inspect why specific sources were selected.

## Future additions

The next useful additions would be more finance, policy, and regional sources, plus Chinese-language feeds if the course demo needs a stronger multilingual angle.
